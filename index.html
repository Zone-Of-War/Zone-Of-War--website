Our Levels Ideology

Level 1

Get introduced to the game. You are dropped into a desert and have to fight agains robots.

Level 2

Abandoned village in the desert. Your goal as the player is to keep exploring the game mechanics.

Level 3

A city witch is filled with robots. Your objective is to kill everyone and get to the next level

Level 4

Abandoned city village. Find the keycard and open the gate, use the car and make the big jump!

Level 5

A village with robots. Find all the generator parts scattered around the level and fix the portal.

Level 6

Military Base. Your goal is to eliminate all of the tanks and enemies and get in to the secret bunker.
